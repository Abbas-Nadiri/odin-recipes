Pizza

Zamn. Now this is a pizza. Thick crust with cheese and pepperoni. Let's make it.

Ingredients

  • 250g pizza dough
  • Mozzarella + Cheddar cheese mix, grated
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni slices

Making the pizza

  1. Preheat the oven to 250 Celsius. Stretch the dough into a circle with a crust.
  2. Spread tomato sauce and sprinkle the cheese over it. Lay pepperoni slices over cheese.
  3. Move pizza to peel/tray and add to oven; bake for 3 minutes.
