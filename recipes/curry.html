Curry

Chicken curry served with rice and leafy greens.

This authentic chicken curry recipe is a banger. This specific style of curry is common in Pakistan where it is a staple food and made in every household. And it's no wonder. The myriad of spices that make up the backbone of this dish's fragrance are fresh and exciting.

Get ready for a one-way trip to flavourtown, which happens to be on a street in Islamabad. Who would've guessed.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg whole chicken, cut into medium-sized pieces
  • 2 large onions, chopped finely
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 50 ml vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic + ginger paste
  • Salt
  • 100 ml yoghurt
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 200 ml water

Making the curry

  1. Heat up the oil in a pan and fry the onions on medium heat till golden-brown.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger paste and fry lightly just until fragrant, then immediately add the chicken to avoid burning it.
  3. Cook the chicken for a minute, then add all of the spices along with salt to taste and fry lightly just till the spices release their fragrance.
  4. Add the chopped tomato and fry until the released water has evaporated.
  5. Add the yoghurt now and stir till combined.
  6. Pour in the water and turn the heat down to low. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  7. Serve with plain naan or rice.
